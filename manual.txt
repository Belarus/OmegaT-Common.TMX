##Ubuntu

1. Change URL in get.sh

##BSPlayer

1. Download free version from http://www.bsplayer.com/
2. Install
3. Copy lang/English.lng and lang/Belarusian.lng into tmp-manual/bsplayer

##Opera

1. Download Linux .tar version from http://www.opera.com
2. Copy share/opera/locale/en/en.lng and share/opera/locale/be/be.lng into tmp-manual/opera

##Drupal

1. Run from IDE: "load.Drupal 7.x"

##Wordpress

1. Get from https://codex.wordpress.org/WordPress_in_Your_Language
2. Extract wp-content/languages/be_BY.po into tmp-manual/wordpress
